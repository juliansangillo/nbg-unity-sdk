Description

An adapter for Unity's RaycastHit. A RaycastHit, or more commonly known as a 'hit', is a struct that provides details on a collider that was hit by a Raycast. For the sake of convenience, this adapter obtains the GameObject the collider was attached to, since that is more valuable data in most circumstances.
